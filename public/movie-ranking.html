Lindsey's Movie Love List

The Best Movies I can think of in 5 minutes

Princess Mononoke

The writing, directing, and artwork are phenomenal. The message was compelling and every character had nuance, even the villains.

The Terminator

Classic. The directing, script and acting are all iconic. Who can forget The Terminator?

Everything Everywhere All At Once

I loved the message, and that it was an Asian-led film at its heart about a family of immigrants as they travel through time and parallel dimensions. Wicked fun.
